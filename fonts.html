Movie posters, magazines, animations, films, all have fonts that represent their own themes. Fonts that build up fear indeed also do exist, with some being very stoic and strong in serif, to if overdone, perhaps tacky yet illustrative fonts. For example, the movie poster on the left uses serif fonts, yet along with the use of bright red color, it represents bloody horror theme that builds the overall atmosphere.
